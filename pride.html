Pride and Prejudice

by Jane Austen

"Pride and Prejudice" is a powerful romantic and spiritual novel written by Nimra Ahmed. The story revolves around Haya, a young, modern girl whose life takes a turn when she receives a scholarship to Turkey. Through suspense, secrets, and soul-searching, the novel beautifully weaves themes of faith, modesty, personal growth, and love. It has been one of the most beloved Urdu novels of the modern era.
